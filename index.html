Cascade and specificity is how CSS decides who is stronger in the case of a conflict.

Cascade Rule: Later styles replace earlier ones.

Specificity Rule: More specific selectors win out over less specific ones and are more important than later selectors.

padding: 10px 15px 15px 5px; is equivalent to:

padding-top: 10px;
padding-right: 15px;
padding-bottom: 15px;
padding-left: 5px;

background: red url(bg-graphic.png) 10px 10px repeat-x fixed;

background-color: red;
background-image: url(bg-graphic.png);
background-position: 10px 10px;
background-repeat: repeat-x;
background-attachment: fixed;
